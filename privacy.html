Link — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Link does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Link has no servers, no accounts, and no databases. All information you enter — contacts, events, notes, and your profile — is stored exclusively in your iPhone's local storage and never leaves your device.

Contacts

If you choose to import contacts from your phone, that information is copied into the app's local storage on your device only. It is never uploaded, synced, or shared with anyone.

Analytics and tracking

Link does not use analytics tools, crash reporters, advertising networks, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

No account required

Link works entirely without creating an account. There is no sign-in and no registration.

Data deletion

Deleting the app from your device permanently and immediately removes all data associated with it. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children's privacy

Link does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. We will never introduce data collection without updating this policy first.
