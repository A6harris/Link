Link — Privacy Policy Last updated: May 19, 2026 Link does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter in the app — contacts, events, notes, and your profile — is stored exclusively on your device using your iPhone's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server, and no data ever leaves your device. Contacts If you choose to import contacts from your phone, that information is copied into the app's local storage on your device. It is never uploaded or shared. No analytics or tracking Link does not use any analytics tools, crash reporters, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect data. No account required Link works entirely without an account. We have no servers and no databases that could hold your information. Data deletion Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all data associated with it. Contact Questions? Email: adenboone@gmail.com
